Reachloop

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

Reachloop ("App") is a personal CRM application developed by Ozan Bilgili ("Developer", "we"). Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected.

1. Data We Collect

Reachloop collects and stores the following data types on your device to provide core app functionality:

Data Type Purpose Sharing
Contact information Contact management Not shared
Name Contact management Not shared
Email address Contact management Not shared
Phone number Contact management Not shared

2. Data Storage

All your personal data is synced only to your private iCloud account using Apple iCloud (CloudKit). Your data is not stored on our servers.

  • Data is stored on your device and in your iCloud Private Database.
  • iCloud sync is subject to Apple's encryption standards.
  • The Developer cannot access your data in iCloud Private Database.

3. Third-Party Services

Adapty

We use Adapty for subscription management. Adapty processes only an anonymous user ID and purchase information to verify your subscription status. It does not have access to your contact data.

Apple CloudKit

Your data is stored on Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Tracking and Advertising

Reachloop:

  • Does not track users.
  • Does not display advertisements.
  • Does not use third-party analytics tools.
  • Does not share your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Notifications

The app uses Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) to send reminder notifications. Notification content is generated on your device and is not processed by any server.

6. Contacts Access

Reachloop may request access to your iOS contacts. This access is used only to import contact information into the app. Your contacts data is not shared with third parties or sent to our servers.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Delete individual contacts from within the app.
  • Remove the app from your device to delete local data.
  • Manage your iCloud data via Settings > iCloud > Storage.

8. Children's Privacy

Reachloop is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be published on this page.
